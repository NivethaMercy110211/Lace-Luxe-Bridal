Know Your Fabric

Fabric & Material Guide

Understanding your lace base helps you choose the perfect combination of weight, drape, and texture.

Silk Tulle

Feather-light and whisper-soft. The most luxurious veil base with a natural sheen that photographs beautifully.

Nylon Tulle

Crisp body and structure. Excellent for full, dramatic veils with strong shape retention throughout the day.

Organza

Semi-sheer with a stiff, formal finish. Ideal for structured cathedral veils that hold their form at the altar.

Illusion Net

Ultra-fine mesh that nearly disappears on the face. Perfect for blusher veils and soft face-framing styles.
