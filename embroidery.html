How It Works

The Design Process

Creating your custom embroidered veil is a collaborative journey. Here's how we bring your vision to life.

Initial Consultation

Meet with your assigned embroidery artist via video or in-studio to discuss your vision, dress style, and wedding theme.

Design Sketch & Approval

Our artists produce a hand-drawn digital sketch of your embroidery layout for review. Unlimited revisions until perfect.

Material Selection

Choose from our curated thread, crystal, and pearl library. We source exclusively from French and Italian suppliers.

Handcraft & QC

Your veil is embroidered by hand over 4–8 weeks, then inspected stitch-by-stitch by our quality team.

Delivery

Presented in a signature Lace Luxe box with a care card and the artist's certificate of authenticity.
