The Beginning

A Thread Through Time

Lace Luxe Bridal was founded in 2018 by Elena Marchetti, a third-generation lacemaker whose grandmother taught her the art of Venetian needle lace at age seven.

2010 — The Spark

Elena graduates from the Istituto d'Arte in Venice, specializing in traditional lace-making techniques spanning 400 years of European craft.

2015 — First Bride

Elena creates a cathedral veil for a childhood friend's wedding. The photographs go viral, receiving 2 million impressions in 3 days.

2018 — Lace Luxe is Born

The studio opens in San Francisco's Noe Valley neighborhood with 3 artisans and a dream to redefine the bridal veil experience.

2022 — Try at Home Launch

We launch our revolutionary try-at-home program, shipping 800 kits in the first month alone and transforming the online bridal market.

2025 — Global Reach

Now serving brides in 42 countries with a team of 28 master artisans. 1,200+ veils created. Every single one still made by hand.

What We Stand For

Our Values

Artisan First

Every piece is hand-crafted by skilled artisans. We never use machine-made lace or automated embroidery.

Sustainability

Zero-waste production, organic packaging, and fabrics sourced only from mills with certified ethical labor practices.

Inclusivity

Every bride deserves extraordinary. Our try-at-home program breaks down barriers of geography and budget access.

Integrity

Transparent pricing. Honest timelines. Unconditional guarantee. We say what we mean and deliver what we promise.
