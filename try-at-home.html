Questions Answered

Try-at-Home FAQ

The try-at-home program is completely free. There is no deposit, no credit card required to reserve your kit, and no obligation to purchase. You only pay for free 2-way shipping.
You can select up to 4 veil styles per kit. We recommend choosing a variety of lengths and embellishments to properly compare your options.
You have 5 full calendar days from the day your kit arrives. We'll send you a friendly reminder email on day 4. Simply drop the box at any post office or schedule a pickup by day 5.
Accidents happen! Each kit includes a small accidental damage allowance. We only charge for damage beyond normal wear — and we always communicate transparently before any charges.
Our try-at-home collection features our most popular ready-to-order styles. Custom embroidery options are available to add after purchase. Your stylist can show you embroidery samples during the kit delivery.
Simply contact us via email, phone, or our website consultation form. Your assigned stylist will guide you through the customization process — length, fabric, embellishment, and delivery timeline.
